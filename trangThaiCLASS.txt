KE HOACH CHUAN HOA STATE MACHINE CHO LopHoc VA DangKyLopHoc

Muc tieu
- Sua loi lech nghia giua model, controller, view, chat, dashboard.
- Tao 1 nguon su that duy nhat cho trangThai.
- Dinh nghia ro rang chuyen trang thai nao duoc phep, ai duoc phep chuyen.
- Dat quy tac de cac module moi khong dung so "magic number" nua.

Van de hien tai
- LopHoc dang bi hieu khac nhau giua dashboard va cac trang client/admin.
- DangKyLopHoc dang bi hieu khac nhau giua model, man hinh hoc vien, checkout, chat, cong no.
- Nhieu noi dang hard-code [1, 2], [4], "dang hoc", "cho thanh toan" ma khong thong qua constant chung.
- Khi phat trien tiep rat de lap lai loi vi khong co tai lieu state machine chuan.

==================================================
1. NGUYEN TAC CHUAN HOA
==================================================

1. Moi bang co trangThai phai co:
- 1 bo constant duy nhat trong Model.
- 1 ham tra nhan hien thi.
- 1 ham tra badge/color neu can.
- 1 bo helper/scopes cho nghiep vu.

2. Khong dung so truc tiep trong controller/view/javascript.
- Khong viet whereIn('trangThai', [1, 2]).
- Phai viet bang constant hoac scope ro nghia.

3. Quyen va nghiep vu phai dua tren "nghia", khong dua tren "so".
- Vi du: canRegister(), canJoinChat(), canViewSchedule(), isActiveForTeaching().

4. Neu doi nghia trangThai:
- Cap nhat Model.
- Cap nhat service nghiep vu.
- Cap nhat controller.
- Cap nhat view.
- Cap nhat test.
- Cap nhat tai lieu nay.

==================================================
2. STATE MACHINE MUC TIEU CHO LopHoc
==================================================

Ten bang: LopHoc
Muc dich: quan ly vong doi cua 1 lop tu luc tao den luc ket thuc.

De xuat bo trang thai chuan
- 0 = NHAP / SAP_MO
  Nghia: lop da duoc tao, chua mo tuyen sinh.
  Hien thi: Sap mo.

- 1 = DANG_TUYEN_SINH
  Nghia: cho phep hoc vien dang ky.
  Hien thi: Dang mo dang ky.

- 2 = CHOT_DANH_SACH
  Nghia: ngung nhan dang ky moi, cho khai giang hoac cho sap lop.
  Hien thi: Da dong dang ky.

- 3 = DANG_HOC
  Nghia: lop da bat dau hoc.
  Hien thi: Dang hoc.

- 4 = DA_KET_THUC
  Nghia: lop da hoc xong.
  Hien thi: Da ket thuc.

- 5 = DA_HUY
  Nghia: lop khong con gia tri van hanh.
  Hien thi: Da huy.

Chuyen trang thai hop le
- NHAP / SAP_MO -> DANG_TUYEN_SINH
- DANG_TUYEN_SINH -> CHOT_DANH_SACH
- DANG_TUYEN_SINH -> DA_HUY
- CHOT_DANH_SACH -> DANG_HOC
- CHOT_DANH_SACH -> DA_HUY
- DANG_HOC -> DA_KET_THUC
- DANG_HOC -> DA_HUY

Khong cho phep
- DA_KET_THUC -> DANG_TUYEN_SINH
- DA_HUY -> quay lai trang thai dang van hanh neu khong co quy trinh mo lai ro rang

Quy tac nghiep vu
- Chi LopHoc o DANG_TUYEN_SINH moi duoc dang ky moi.
- LopHoc o CHOT_DANH_SACH khong nhan dang ky moi nhung van cho phep hoc vu thao tac noi bo.
- LopHoc o DANG_HOC moi duoc mo chat day/hoc chinh thuc, diem danh, day bu.
- LopHoc o DA_KET_THUC chi duoc xem lich su.

Goi y dong bo code
- Dashboard "lop dang hoat dong" nen tinh tren DANG_TUYEN_SINH + CHOT_DANH_SACH + DANG_HOC neu muon KPI van hanh.
- Neu KPI chi tinh "dang giang day" thi chi lay DANG_HOC.
- Client class detail phai map dung nhan theo constant moi.

==================================================
3. STATE MACHINE MUC TIEU CHO DangKyLopHoc
==================================================

Ten bang: DangKyLopHoc
Muc dich: quan ly hanh trinh cua 1 hoc vien trong 1 lop.

De xuat bo trang thai chuan
- 0 = CHO_THANH_TOAN
  Nghia: vua dang ky, chua thanh toan du de giu cho.
  Hien thi: Cho thanh toan.

- 1 = DA_XAC_NHAN
  Nghia: da dat dieu kien tai chinh/hoc vu de duoc giu cho.
  Hien thi: Da xac nhan.

- 2 = DANG_HOC
  Nghia: hoc vien dang hoc trong lop.
  Hien thi: Dang hoc.

- 3 = TAM_DUNG_NO_HOC_PHI
  Nghia: tam khoa quyen hoc vi cong no.
  Hien thi: Tam dung do no hoc phi.

- 4 = BAO_LUU
  Nghia: hoc vien duoc bao luu theo quy trinh.
  Hien thi: Bao luu.

- 5 = HOAN_THANH
  Nghia: hoc vien hoc xong lop.
  Hien thi: Hoan thanh.

- 6 = HUY
  Nghia: huy dang ky, khong con theo lop nay.
  Hien thi: Da huy.

Chuyen trang thai hop le
- CHO_THANH_TOAN -> DA_XAC_NHAN
- CHO_THANH_TOAN -> HUY
- DA_XAC_NHAN -> DANG_HOC
- DA_XAC_NHAN -> HUY
- DANG_HOC -> TAM_DUNG_NO_HOC_PHI
- DANG_HOC -> BAO_LUU
- DANG_HOC -> HOAN_THANH
- DANG_HOC -> HUY
- TAM_DUNG_NO_HOC_PHI -> DANG_HOC
- BAO_LUU -> DANG_HOC

Quy tac nghiep vu
- CHO_THANH_TOAN:
  Duoc xem hoa don.
  Chua duoc xem lich hoc chinh thuc.
  Chua duoc tham gia chat hoc tap.

- DA_XAC_NHAN:
  Duoc giu cho.
  Duoc xuat hien trong danh sach lop cua toi.
  Co the duoc tham gia chat pre-class neu trung tam muon.

- DANG_HOC:
  Duoc xem lich hoc, diem danh, chat, tai lieu.

- TAM_DUNG_NO_HOC_PHI:
  Van giu lich su dang ky.
  Co the bi khoa mot phan quyen hoc tap.
  Chi mo lai khi cong no hop le.

- BAO_LUU:
  Khong tinh vao si so hoc thuc te.
  Khong diem danh.

- HOAN_THANH:
  Chi doc lich su.

- HUY:
  Khong con quyen hoc.

==================================================
4. QUY TAC DONG BO GIUA LopHoc VA DangKyLopHoc
==================================================

1. Khi hoc vien tu dang ky tren website
- Tao DangKyLopHoc = CHO_THANH_TOAN.
- Tao HoaDon = CHUA_THANH_TOAN.

2. Khi thanh toan du
- Neu LopHoc chua bat dau: CHO_THANH_TOAN -> DA_XAC_NHAN.
- Neu LopHoc da vao trang thai DANG_HOC: CHO_THANH_TOAN -> DANG_HOC.

3. Khi LopHoc chuyen tu CHOT_DANH_SACH -> DANG_HOC
- Toan bo DangKyLopHoc = DA_XAC_NHAN cua lop se duoc chuyen sang DANG_HOC.

4. Khi hoa don qua han
- Neu hoc vien dang hoc: DANG_HOC -> TAM_DUNG_NO_HOC_PHI.

5. Khi hoc vien thanh toan no
- TAM_DUNG_NO_HOC_PHI -> DANG_HOC.

6. Khi LopHoc DA_KET_THUC
- DangKyLopHoc dang DANG_HOC cua lop -> HOAN_THANH.

7. Khi LopHoc DA_HUY
- DangKyLopHoc chua hoan thanh -> HUY hoac xu ly chuyen lop theo quy trinh rieng.

==================================================
5. QUY TAC TRUY CAP THEO TRANG THAI
==================================================

Dang ky moi
- Chi cho phep khi LopHoc = DANG_TUYEN_SINH.

Trang "Lop hoc cua toi"
- Hien thi tab theo nhom:
  Cho xu ly: CHO_THANH_TOAN, DA_XAC_NHAN
  Dang hoc: DANG_HOC, TAM_DUNG_NO_HOC_PHI, BAO_LUU
  Lich su: HOAN_THANH, HUY

Lich hoc
- Chi cho phep xem khi DangKyLopHoc = DANG_HOC.
- Co the cho DA_XAC_NHAN xem lich sap toi neu muon, nhung phai thong nhat toan he thong.

Chat lop
- Join room: DA_XAC_NHAN hoac DANG_HOC.
- Gui tin: DANG_HOC.
- TAM_DUNG_NO_HOC_PHI: tuy chinh sach, mac dinh khong gui tin.

Diem danh
- Chi tao/ghi nhan cho DANG_HOC.

Tai lieu hoc tap
- Chi cho DANG_HOC.

==================================================
6. KE HOACH SUA LOI TRONG CODE
==================================================

Giai doan 1 - Chot tai lieu va constant
- Chot nghia chinh thuc cua LopHoc va DangKyLopHoc theo tai lieu nay.
- Tao constant moi trong Model.
- Tao helper nhu:
  canRegister()
  canJoinChat()
  canSendChat()
  canViewSchedule()
  isOperational()

Giai doan 2 - Loai bo magic number
- Tim toan bo noi dung dang dung [1, 2], [4], "dang hoc" trong controller/view/service.
- Thay bang constant va helper.
- Dac biet kiem tra:
  CourseController
  StudentController
  ChatAccessService
  DashboardService
  LopHocController
  cac view client/admin

Giai doan 3 - Migration du lieu
- Lap script mapping du lieu cu sang state machine moi.
- Mapping de xuat cho DangKyLopHoc:
  Neu trangThai cu = 1 va HoaDon chua thanh toan du -> CHO_THANH_TOAN
  Neu trangThai cu = 1 va HoaDon da thanh toan du -> DA_XAC_NHAN hoac DANG_HOC tuy LopHoc
  Neu trangThai cu = 2 -> can review theo du lieu thuc te vi hien dang bi dung lech nghia
- Mapping de xuat cho LopHoc:
  Chuyen cac record dang dung 4 = DANG_HOC sang constant moi neu doi ma so

Giai doan 4 - Dong bo UI/UX
- Admin list/show/edit LopHoc.
- Client chi tiet lop.
- Client "lop hoc cua toi".
- Client lich hoc.
- Badge/thong bao/chat.

Giai doan 5 - Dong bo service va automation
- Finance: recalculate hoa don.
- Cong no qua han.
- Chat access.
- Dashboard KPI.
- Cron/job cap nhat trang thai theo ngay bat dau/ngay ket thuc neu can.

Giai doan 6 - Test
- Test dang ky moi.
- Test thanh toan du.
- Test qua han hoc phi.
- Test vao chat.
- Test xem lich hoc.
- Test lop ket thuc.

==================================================
7. QUY TAC PHAT TRIEN TRONG TUONG LAI
==================================================

Bat buoc
- Moi trangThai moi phai co tai lieu.
- Moi transition moi phai co test.
- Moi view chi hien thi label qua accessor/helper, khong so sanh so truc tiep.
- Moi query nghiep vu phai doc bang ten nghia, khong doc bang so.

Khuyen nghi
- Tao enum hoac Value Object neu du an len version PHP phu hop.
- Tao file StateMap hoac service rieng cho state machine.
- Tach "trangThai nghiep vu" va "trangThai hien thi" neu can.
